Executive Brief

From phone calls to a shop that runs on data.

Same kitchen. Same riders. A smarter way to take every order — and to learn from it.

Om · Febin · Alok · Guru · Rahul
The Problem

Today, every order rides on a phone call.

  • Taken by hand — missed calls and the wrong pizza at the door.
  • Maths in someone's head — discounts and 18% GST become billing errors and refunds.
  • Nothing is recorded — you plan the next week on memory, not numbers.
≈ 60–90 staff-minutes lost every day
The Solution

One guided order, always correct.

You order
in plain language, soon by voice
AI extracts
name, qty, base, pizza, topping
Code prices it
validation, discount, GST — never the AI
Bill + logged
correct to the paisa, saved for analytics
The AI understands your order. Our code computes every rupee.
The bulk discount and 18% GST are calculated in code — so the bill is always exact, never guessed by the AI.
